One of the very few, but most beautiful rainforests of Sri Lanka. Sinharaja rain forest is a sanctuary to many varieties of plants, birds and animals that take refuge in the moist and murky rain forest.

Hiking through this piece of Eden will leave you truly mesmerized and in awe, you may follow the gentle streams that lead to exhilarating waterfalls, while a cool breeze dances with the trees.

The forest has a rich and vibrant history which dates back 1000s of years. The Sinharaja rain forest however has been diluted from its former glory, throughout the years due to colonizers and different industries taking up parts of land. Yet the small piece that remains, still stands as symbol for Sri Lankas beauty and grace.
